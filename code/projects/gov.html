.01 Overview

While I can't get into the details of this project, I'm happy to talk through the process that got it done.

I worked across several teams on this project, the first being an awesome UX team. We conducted research with our target audience and developed a template-based approach to wireframes.

I also supported the visual design team with creation of pixel-perfect mockups and interaction design. In addition, I edited images for your final site and conducted visual QA of the build.

Beyond design, I also flexed to support the content strategy of our site. I created high level guidance and deliverables for our client to rely on post handoff.
